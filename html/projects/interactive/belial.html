Belial is an innovative software that offers a revolution in performance and interactive art. 

    The software is based on algorithms and allows the creation and generation of music with or without human participation. Belial offers an accessible set of tools for sound art performance and its aim is to free musicians from the limitations of traditional software. 

    The software offers the freedom to experiment and explore the possibilities of real-time sound generation, and it is suitable for professionals and beginners alike. Belial has a simple interface and intuitive functionality, making it a worthy tool for anyone interested in sound art.

    Investing in Belial is an investment in the future of sound art, as it offers cutting edge technology and revolutionary design. 

    The programme author hopes that Belial will inspire new musical experiences and looks forward to collaborating with a wide audience.
