Epatage, challenge, playing on the edge. Exploiting his image, multimedia conceptual artist Ilya Minin tells modest workers from the association "Say 中刄长下" about the principles of his craft.

Art is the artist's weapon against the imperfections of this world. Ilya reveals the problems that face the contemporary art world in a comprehensive way.
