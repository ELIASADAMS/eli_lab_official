    A mesmerising electro-acoustic journey through soundscapes and sonic textures, Eli's new album "Lab Announcement 3" is a work of art.
    The eccentric musician has developed a bold, unpolished and compelling soundtrack using his unique method of working with graphics, sparse analogue instrumentation, processed field recordings, microchromatics and granular synthesis.

    The creator of the gallerave genre, Eli has paved the way for a fresh sound that seamlessly blends electroacoustics, dance music and computational formulas. The complex and layered soundscapes of 'Lab Announcement 3' demonstrate his mastery of natural magnetic forces.

    "Lab Announcement 3" is a creative and daring piece of electroacoustic craftsmanship, and it is clear that Eli continues to push the boundaries of the genre. This album is a must for anyone looking for a immersive and transformative experience in modern music.
